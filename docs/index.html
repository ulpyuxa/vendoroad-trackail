Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Trackail (the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide: We collect shipment tracking numbers and carrier names that you manually enter into the App to provide tracking services.

Automatically Collected Information: The App may access your network state to determine if it can sync data. We do not collect personal identifiers, location data, or contact lists.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To fetch and update the status of your shipments.
  • To provide local notifications regarding shipment progress.
  • To improve App performance and user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses 17TRACK API to retrieve shipment information. When you track a package, the tracking number and carrier code are sent to 17TRACK. Please refer to 17TRACK's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

4. Data Storage

Your tracking data is stored locally on your device using a secure database (Room Database). We do not upload your personal tracking list to our own servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: simon@trackail.com

隐私权政策

最后更新日期：2026年4月13日

Trackail（以下简称“本应用”）致力于保护您的个人隐私。本隐私政策说明了您在使用我们的移动应用时，我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

您提供的信息： 我们收集您手动输入的物流单号和快递公司名称，以便为您提供追踪服务。

自动收集的信息： 本应用可能会访问您的网络状态，以判断是否可以同步数据。我们不会收集个人身份识别码、位置信息或联系人列表。

2. 我们如何使用您的信息

  • 获取并更新您的物流状态。
  • 提供物流进度的本地通知。
  • 提升应用性能和用户体验。

3. 第三方服务

本应用使用 17TRACK API 获取物流信息。当您追踪包裹时，追踪单号和快递公司代码将被发送至 17TRACK。请参阅 17TRACK 隐私政策 以了解他们处理数据的方式。

4. 数据存储

您的追踪数据使用安全数据库（Room Database）本地存储在您的设备上。我们不会将您的个人追踪列表上传到我们的服务器。

5. 儿童隐私

我们的应用不面向 13 岁以下的儿童。我们不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人身份信息。

我们可能会不时更新隐私政策。建议您定期查看此页面以了解任何变动。
